How Stock Chances Work

Lower numbers = Higher stock chances! The stock chance value represents how likely an item is to appear in the event shop.

Common (1-5)

Items with stock chances between 1-5 are very likely to be in stock and replenish quickly.

Uncommon (6-15)

Items with stock chances between 6-15 are moderately common and should be available most of the time.

Rare (16-30)

Items with stock chances between 16-30 are less common and may take longer to restock.

Very Rare (31-60)

Items with stock chances between 31-60 are very rare and may only appear occasionally.
